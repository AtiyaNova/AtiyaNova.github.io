ABOUT
Currently I'm pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at Ontario Tech University. I majored in Game Development and Entrepreneurship, with a minor in Game Programming.
Ever since I was a child I've dreamt about being a game developer. I started by making rudimentary text adventure games, and as time went on I discovered my passion for programming and the arts.

These days I've been focusing on completing my Masters while juggling solo projects. Please feel free to contact me regarding any work opportunities.
