ABOUT

My name is Atiya Nova.

I'm pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at Ontario Tech University. I majored in Game Development and Entrepreneurship, with a minor in Game Programming.


I coded this website in HTML and CSS.

Ever since I was a kid I dreamt of being a game developer. I started by making rudimentary text adventure games, and as time went on I learned how to do more and more. I discovered my passion for programming and art.

Some of the games I've worked on were featured at various showcases.
I cover my experience and work in further detail on this site, along with on my linkedin, CV, etc. Please contact me if you'd like to inquire about work opportunities.
